What is the purpose and legal basis for processing your personal data?

The basis for processing your personal data is a cooperation agreement with the Partner for services provided by Uber in Poland. Your personal data is processed by LDB. for the following purposes:

  • Conclusion and performance of a contract between us (Article 6 paragraph (1b) GDPR - execution of the contract)
  • Contact regarding the contract between us (Article 6(1b) GDPR - execution of the contract)
  • Handling your enquiries and complaints, including the resolution of technical problems (Article 6(1) c of the GDR - performance of our legal obligations)
  • Accounting (Article 6(1)(c) of the GDP - performance of our legal obligations)
  • To conduct judicial and administrative proceedings (Article 6(1) f, GDP - our legally justified business)
  • For marketing purposes (Article 6(1) a GDP - consent)
  • The possibilities of using the Uber application for all its functionalities, details can be found under the link; https://privacy.uber.com/policy/
  • Possible other circumstances, resulting from the cooperation process and necessary for its legally correct implementation
